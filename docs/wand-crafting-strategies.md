## 2. STRATEGY COMPARISON

### Strategy A: "Magic Base + Regal" (Your Current Approach)

**Process:**
1. Farm for a magic base with `+5 to Level of all Chaos Spell Skills`
2. Craft/roll for a good prefix (e.g., T2 Chaos Damage) on the magic
3. Use Regal Orb to add a random suffix → convert to rare
4. Craft remaining mods (exalts, annulments, rerolls as needed)

**Cost Estimate:**
- Magic bases with +5 skill: ~5 divines each (rare drop)
- Tries until good prefix on magic: ~5 alts + 1 aug (assume 10 tries) = ~1 divine
- Regal Orb: 1 divine
- Post-regal crafting (annuls, exalts, crafted mod): ~30–50 divines
- **Total: ~40–60 divines**

**Pros:**
- Guarantees you start with +5 skill (highest tier)
- Locks in a good prefix pre-regal
- Fewer brute-force crafting steps

**Cons:**
- Magic base hunting is tedious
- Regal outcomes are random—might brick with an unwanted suffix
- Still need 30–50 divines post-regal for final suffixes

**Verdict:** Good if you have access to farming for +5 bases. Saves currency on the spell-level suffix step.

---

### Strategy B: "White Base Essences"

**Process:**
1. Buy/farm cheap white Attuned Wand bases: ~0.2 div each
2. Use Essence of Anguish (chaos damage) + 2x other essences (spell damage, fire damage)
3. Roll & annul down to 3 prefixes
4. Exalt + craft suffixes

**Cost Estimate:**
- White base: 0.2 div
- Essences: ~2 div total (buying if not farming)
- Rolling for 3P (assume ~20 tries, lots of annuls): ~15–20 divines
- Exalting & crafting suffixes: ~20–30 divines
- **Total: ~40–55 divines**

**Pros:**
- No need to hunt magic bases
- Essences guarantee one specific prefix (can use Anguish for guaranteed Chaos)
- More deterministic path to 3 prefixes if you have essence access

**Cons:**
- Essences can be expensive or require farming
- Still need annuls to clean up unwanted prefixes (RNG-heavy)
- Suffix crafting is still brute-force exalt

**Verdict:** Good if you have essence access. Similar cost to Magic Base, slightly more transparent.

### Strategy D: "Pure Exalt Spam" (Brute Force)

**Process:**
1. Start with white base (~0.2 div)
2. Exalt until you hit a good prefix
3. Craft bench or exalt remaining prefixes
4. Exalt until suffixes are right

**Cost Estimate:**
- Base: 0.2 div
- Prefixes: You need 3 specific mods. Assuming ~1–2% spawn rate per mod at a given tier:
  - Hit T1 Chaos: ~100 exalts = ~100 div
  - Hit T1 Spell: ~100 exalts = ~100 div
  - Hit T2/T3 Fire: ~20–50 exalts = ~20–50 div
  - Annuls to remove bad prefixes: ~30–50 div
- Suffixes: Exalt spam + annuls: ~100+ divines
- **Total: 350–500 divines** (EXPENSIVE)

**Pros:**
- Theoretically works with infinite currency
- No dependency on farming or essence access

**Cons:**
- Extremely expensive
- High variance—could take 500+ exalts to hit the right rolls
- RNG-intensive (unfun)

**Verdict:** Not recommended unless divines are abundant. Save for other characters.

---

### Strategy E: "Hybrid: Essence + Exalt" (RECOMMENDED)

**Process:**
1. Start with white base (~0.2 div)
2. Use Essence of Anguish to guarantee T1–T2 Chaos Damage prefix
3. Craft Spell Damage or Fire Damage on the bench if needed (uses a crafted mod slot)
4. Exalt 1–2 times to hit the remaining prefixes (or bench craft)
5. Exalt suffixes, annul bad ones, lock good ones with suffixes can't be changed

**Cost Estimate:**
- Base: 0.2 div
- Essence: ~1–2 div (Anguish is common)
- Crafting mods on bench: ~2–3 div (multiple crafts)
- Exalts for prefixes (fewer needed if using bench): ~10–20 div
- Suffix crafting (exalt + annul cycle): ~30–50 div
- Suffix lock craft: ~5–10 div
- **Total: 50–90 divines**

**Pros:**
- Essence guarantees one top-tier prefix
- Bench crafting fills gaps cheaply
- Fewer exalts needed than pure brute-force
- Suffix lock craft protects finished suffixes (stops waste)

**Cons:**
- Requires understanding of bench crafting mechanics
- Still uses significant exalts
- Essence farming/buying time

**Verdict:** Best balance of cost, speed, and predictability.

## 4. DETAILED WALKTHROUGH: Strategy E

### Phase 1: Prefixes (Target: 3 specific high-tier damage mods)

**Step 1.1:** Buy cheap white Attuned Wand base (~0.2 div)

**Step 1.2:** Use Essence of Anguish
- Guarantees a chaos damage prefix (T1 or T2 depending on essence tier)
- Cost: ~1–2 divines
- Result: Base now has 1/3 prefixes locked (Chaos Damage)

**Step 1.3:** Exalt once (~1 div)
- Goal: Hit Spell Damage (T1) or accept T2 and craft it later
- If you hit Spell Damage tier 1–3: keep it
- If you hit something else (unwanted prefix or suffix): Annul that mod (~1 div), and repeat exalting

**Step 1.4:** Bench craft or exalt for P3
- If you've hit Chaos + Spell Damage: Exalt or craft the third prefix
  - Crafting Fire Damage on bench: ~2 divines + 1 orb of augmentation (cheap)
  - Exalting again: ~1 divine, but RNG (might hit suffix)
- **Optimal:** Use bench craft to add Fire Damage as a non-random mod. Cost: ~3 divines total for bench (multiple uses)

**End of Phase 1:** You have 3 prefixes. Cost so far: ~6–10 divines.

---

### Phase 2: Suffixes (Target: 3 specific high-tier crit/level mods)

**Step 2.1:** Lock prefixes
- Use "Prefixes cannot be changed" craft (bench): ~5–10 divines
- This ensures exalting only adds suffixes

**Step 2.2:** Exalt for suffixes (repeat until you have 3)
- Exalt 1 (~1 div): Goal = Spell Level (+1 or +2)
  - If hit: lock it with "Suffixes cannot be changed" + add a second suffix via craft
  - If miss: Annul (~1 div) and retry
- Exalt 2 (~1 div): Goal = Crit Hit Chance
  - Similar retry logic
- Exalt 3 (~1 div): Goal = Crit Damage Bonus
  - If miss, annul and retry

**Step 2.3:** Estimate tries
- Spell Level (+1 or higher): ~8% spawn rate → expect ~12 exalts = ~12 div
- Crit Chance (any tier OK): ~20%+ spawn rate → expect ~5 exalts = ~5 div
- Crit Damage Bonus (any tier OK): ~20%+ spawn rate → expect ~5 exalts = ~5 div
- Annuls for failed attempts: ~10–20 div
- Suffix lock/unlock crafts: ~10–15 div

**End of Phase 2:** You have all 6 mods. Cost so far: ~60–80 divines.

---

### Phase 3: Fine-tuning (Optional)

**Step 3.1:** If suffixes are low tier (e.g., Crit Chance at 34-39 instead of 54-59)
- Option A: Live with it (wand still works, just less crit)
- Option B: Recraft that suffix (Annul + Exalt): ~10–20 div per suffix

## 7. SPELL LEVEL SUFFIX: Magic Base Advantage

The main advantage of starting with a magic base (with +5 skill) is that you **skip the suffix RNG** for spell levels. Instead of:
- Exalting 15–20 times hoping to hit `+1 Spell Level` (~8% spawn)

You only need to exalt 2 times for the other two suffixes (crit chance + crit damage), which have much higher spawn rates (~20%+).

**Cost savings:** ~8–12 divines (fewer exalts + annuls)

**This is why Magic Base + Regal is so popular—it cuts the single worst RNG bottleneck.**

### Spell Level Tier Trade-off

**Key insight:** You don't need +5 Spell Level to deal damage. Options:
- +1 Chaos Spell Skills: Hexblast/Despair +1
- +2 Spell Skills: All spells +2
- +5 Chaos Spell Skills (perfect): All chaos skills +5

The curve is:
- +1 → +2: ~50% more damage (scaling)
- +2 → +3: ~33% more damage
- +4 → +5: ~20% more damage

**Cost trade-off:**
- +1 tier: ~8–10 div to hit (high RNG)
- +5 tier (magic base): ~5–10 div to farm

So +1 from exalts ≈ cost of magic base. Farming the base is likely faster.
