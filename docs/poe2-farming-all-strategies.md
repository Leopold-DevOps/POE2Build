## CORE PRINCIPLE: Currency per Hour Formula

```
Divines/Hour = (Drops per activity × Drop value in divines) / Activity time in minutes × 60
```

Example:
- Boss fight drops 2 divines worth of items
- Boss takes 5 minutes (+ 1 min looting/travel = 6 min)
- Divines/hour = (2 div / 6 min) × 60 = **20 div/hour**

## ARCHETYPE 1: SPEED MAPPING (Most Accessible)

**What it is:** Clear maps as fast as possible, loot common drops (maps, currency, vendor trash)

**Divines/hour potential:** 80-120 div/hour (best scaling with skill)

**Time per activity:** 2-3 minutes per map

**Startup cost:** 0-20 divines (depends on current gear)

### How It Works

1. Run Tier 12+ maps (or highest unlocked)
2. Clear packs with AoE (everything dies in 1-2 hits)
3. Loot while moving (don't wait for corpses)
4. Kill boss in <30 seconds
5. Leave, start new map

### Economics

**Per map drops (conservative):**
- Map drops: 1.2 maps/run (chain maps)
- Chaos orbs + other currency: 0.5 divines value
- Rare drops (vendor): 0.3 divines value
- **Total per map: ~1 divine equivalent**

**With 20-24 maps/hour:** 20-24 div/hour

**Scaling:** Increases with:
- Faster clear speed → more maps/hour
- Better gear → higher-tier maps → better drops
- IIR/IIQ investment → more valuable drops

**Problem:** Entry barrier is gear (need fast, tanky character)

**Solution:** Start with slower, resource-efficient farming (next section)

## ARCHETYPE 5: MARKET FLIPPING (Requires Knowledge)

**What it is:** Buy items cheap, sell high; craft valuable items and flip; arbitrage currency

**Divines/hour potential:** 100-500+ div/hour (highly skill-dependent)

**Time per activity:** 5-30 minutes per flip

**Startup cost:** 50-200 divines

**Skill ceiling:** Extremely high; requires economy knowledge

### How It Works

1. Identify underpriced items (10-20% below market value)
2. Buy them
3. Resell at market rate or craft/enhance them
4. Profit = 1-5 divines per flip

**Example:**
- Buy wand for 40 divines (slightly underpriced)
- Craft it optimally (add 20 divines in mods)
- Sell for 75 divines
- Profit: 15 divines in 30 minutes = 30 div/hour

**Scaling:** Limited by capital and market volume

**Requires:** Deep PoE2 economy knowledge (you don't have this yet at 10 divines)

**Not recommended immediately** (but consider learning after accumulating 200+ divines)

## FORMULA YOU CAN USE FOR ANY STRATEGY

Once you identify a farming method:

```
Div/hour = (Drops per run × Estimated div value) / (Time per run in minutes) × 60
```

**Example:**
- Boss fight takes 6 minutes (5 min fight + 1 min travel/loot)
- Drops: 1 unique (worth 2 div) + 50 chaos (worth 1 div) + 2 maps (worth 0.5 div)
- Total per run: 3.5 divines
- Divines/hour = (3.5 / 6) × 60 = 35 div/hour

**Apply this to every activity you consider.**
