**Your current strategy (Magic Base + Regal):** ~40–60 divines, OPTIMAL if you have +5 base access

---

## Why Magic Base Wins

The spell-level suffix is the biggest RNG bottleneck:
- +1 Spell Level: 8% spawn rate = ~12 exalts = ~12 divines
- +3 Spell Level: 1% spawn rate = ~100 exalts = ~100 divines
- +5 Spell Level (magic base): 0 exalts, farm instead = ~5 divines + time

**The math:** Finding a +5 magic base and regaling it costs ~6–7 divines + 30–60 min farming. Exalting for +1 costs ~12 divines in currency but takes similar time. Either way, starting with +5 saves you ~5–10 divines vs. exalting for +1, plus unlocks the possibility of finding bases with good prefixes pre-regal.

## When to Use Each Strategy

### Use Magic Base + Regal IF:
- You can farm/buy +5 bases reliably
- You're OK with 30–60 min farming time
- You want lowest guaranteed cost
- You prefer deterministic paths (essence/bench costs add up)

### Use Essence + Bench IF:
- Farming magic bases feels unreliable
- You prefer "one-stop" crafting (no base hunting)
- You have bench crafting experience
- You're OK with slightly higher cost (~10–20 div more)

### Use Pure Exalt Spam IF:
- You have 400+ divines to burn
- You want perfect T1 rolls on everything
- You're running low on other crafting projects
- (Generally not recommended)

## Crafting Decisions

### Decision 1: Start White vs. Magic?

| Approach | Cost | Comments |
|---|---|---|
| Magic (+5 skill) | 5 div + farm time | Saves ~8–10 div on exalts; most efficient if available |
| White + essence | ~1–2 div | No farm time, but costs more in exalts/essences |

**Recommendation:** Use Magic if you've seen +5 bases drop; use Essence if they're rare for you.

### Decision 2: Accept mid-tier suffixes or push for T1?

**Mid-tier example:** Crit Damage Bonus T2 (30-34%) instead of T1 (35-39%)

| Choice | DPS Loss | Cost Savings | Speed |
|---|---|---|---|
| Accept T2 suffixes | ~2–3% | ~30–50 div | Fast (fewer exalts) |
| Push for T1 suffixes | — | — | Slow (2–5× exalts) |

For your Sanguine build at 156k DPS, accepting T2 suffixes still gives ~150k DPS.

**Recommendation:** Accept T2–T3 for initial wand; upgrade to T1 later if you need the final 5% damage.

### Decision 3: Annul bad suffixes or reroll the whole item?

**Scenario:** You exalt and hit a suffix you don't want (e.g., Mana Regen instead of Spell Level).

**Option A:** Annul that suffix (1 div), keep other affixes, exalt again (1 div) = 2 div total

**Option B:** Scrap the item and restart = 1 div (white base) + all the crafting costs again

**Recommendation:** Always annul and retry. It's cheaper.

- You might hit T5 Crit Chance (34-39%) instead of T1 (60-73%) = ~3–5% DPS loss

- These losses are acceptable if they save 50+ divines

**For your 156k PoB2 estimate:**
- Real wand (mid-tier suffixes, T1–T2 prefixes): ~150k DPS
- Real wand (all T1): ~160k+ DPS
- Cost difference: ~50–100 divines for that 6–10k DPS gap
