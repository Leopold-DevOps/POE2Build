---

## WHY THIS BUILD AT LEVEL 41?

At level 41, Hexblast becomes available, marking the transition from early-game generic spellcasting to your build's true identity. This spec is designed to:

1. **Establish crit foundation** — Sunder the Flesh (15% base crit) is your damage enabler
2. **Provide survivability** — ~800-1000 life + ES, enough for mid-game maps
3. **Set up scaling** — Gore Spike + life nodes begin the synergy
4. **Prepare for endgame** — Passive tree allocation is reversible; you can respec cleanly at level 60

## CURRENT SETUP EXPLAINED

### Ascendancy Path (4 Points Spent)

**Sanguimancy (Free)** ← Already taken at act start
- Effect: Spells cost life equal to their mana cost
- Reason: This is THE identity of Blood Mage; take it immediately

**Sunder the Flesh (2 Points)**
- Effect: Spells have 15% base critical hit chance
- Reason: Hexblast's native crit is ~5%; this 3× multiplier is your damage foundation
- When to take: At level 41 or sooner (acts 2-3)
- Cost: 2 ascendancy points (easiest nodes on the board)

**Gore Spike (2 Points)**
- Effect: Critical damage bonus scales with maximum life
- Reason: Synergy with Blood Mage (you spend life) and passive tree (life nodes)
- When to take: At level 41 (after Sunder is online)
- Cost: 2 ascendancy points

**NOT YET TAKEN:**
- Whispers of the Flesh (reserve life, curses lose regen) — Level 55+
- Crimson Power (ES counts as life) — Level 70+
- Vitality Siphon (spell leech) — Level 60+ if needed

### Passive Tree (41 Points)

**Priority 1: Life (8 nodes, ~55 life total)**
- Reason: You need 800+ life to survive level 41 content
- Nodes: 116, 2254, 7344, 42077, 57110, 13542, + 2 others
- Budget: 8 points

**Priority 2: Spell Damage (3 nodes, ~40% increased damage)**
- Reason: Hexblast needs scaling; even % damage helps
- Nodes: 2138, 64046, 19125
- Budget: 3 points
- Result: ~20-30k Hexblast DPS at level 41

**Priority 3: Crit Chance (2 nodes, ~20% increased)**
- Reason: Synergy with Sunder's 15% base — every % crit is multiplied by base
- Nodes: 51335, 5501
- Budget: 2 points
- Result: ~30-35% crit chance (good for mid-game)

**Priority 4: Curse Effect (1 node, ~10% increased)**
- Reason: Despair scales with curse effect
- Node: 50485
- Budget: 1 point

**Priority 5: Cast Speed (1 node, ~10% increased)**
- Reason: Hexblast is slow; this helps QoL
- Node: 36302
- Budget: 1 point

**Flex Points (25+ left):**
- Budget: 25 points (don't allocate all yet)
- Reason: Save for level 60 respec or minor efficiency nodes
- Options: More life (always good), more damage if feeling safe, intelligence for gear requirements

**Total Budget Used: ~16 points (leaving 25 flexible)**

### Gem Setup

**Main Skill: Hexblast + Pinpoint Critical**
- Hexblast is your damage
- Pinpoint Critical increases crit chance (early support)
- Alternative supports: None yet (limited gem access at level 41)
- Reason: Simpler is better; scale with passives, not gem links yet

**Curse: Despair (1-link)**
- Despair lowers enemy chaos resistance
- Reason: Hexblast is chaos damage; resistance reduction is huge multiplier
- Setup: Just cast Despair, then Hexblast to blow up cursed targets
- Gem level: Whatever you get from drops (level 15-18 probably)

**Mobility: Blink**
- For repositioning, escape mechanics
- Not critical yet, but essential for comfort

**Later (Level 55+):** Add supports to Hexblast as you unlock socket/gem slots
- Supercritical (crit damage multiplier)
- Magnified Area (AoE increase)
- Rapid Casting (attack/cast speed)

### Gear Strategy

**Philosophy: Don't craft. Just find good rares with life + damage.**

At level 41, farming for perfect gear is wasteful. Instead:

1. **Farm act bosses** for random rare drops
2. **Pick up rares with:**
   - +X to maximum Life (priority)
   - +X% increased Spell Damage (priority)
   - Elemental resistances (cap fire/cold/lightning at 75%)
3. **Don't worry about:** Perfect rolls, specific affixes, crafted mods
4. **Budget:** Use your 10 divines if needed, but avoid it (should find rares)

**Expected gear quality:** All rare, nothing unique, nothing crafted. Good enough for Tier 1-5 maps.

## GAMEPLAY AT LEVEL 41

### Farming Act Bosses (Levels 41-55)

**Strategy:** Repeatedly farm act bosses for:
- Experience (level 41 → 60 is ~2-3M XP, doable in 8-10 hours)
- Drops (rares, occasional good bases)
- Currency (act bosses drop occasional orbs)

**Expected kill time:** 30 seconds to 2 minutes per boss (very fast)

**Estimated exp/hour:** 200-300k exp/hour = 10 hours to level 60

**Recommended bosses:** Whatever act you're in (Dominus in act 3, etc.)

### Farming Maps (Levels 41-60)

**When:** After killing act boss 3-5 times (you'll have 200-300k exp)

**What maps:** Tier 1-3 (easy, drop frequently, good XP/time)

**Expected clear time:** 2-3 minutes per map

**Expected drops:** 1-2 maps per run (sustains mapping)

**Survival notes:**
- Don't face-tank packs (you only have 800 life)
- Use Blink to reposition
- Curse with Despair first, then Hexblast
- If rares one-shot you, backtrack to safety

## COMMON ISSUES & FIXES

### Issue: Dying to packs
- **Fix:** More life nodes (allocate flexible points toward life wheel)
- **Fix:** Improve gear (find rares with +life)
- **Fix:** Use Blink more aggressively (reposition before packs hit)

### Issue: DPS too low, kills take too long
- **Fix:** This is expected at level 41 (20-30k DPS is normal)
- **Fix:** Speed comes from cast speed + crit; allocate more if comfortable
- **Fix:** Wait for level 50+ (more damage naturally)

### Issue: Can't sustain mana
- **Fix:** Blood Mage pays in life, not mana — this is intentional
- **Fix:** If you're dying to life cost, reduce cast spam (kill packs slower)
- **Fix:** At level 60, Crimson Power converts ES into life (more sustain)

### Issue: Gem doesn't exist or isn't available
- **Fix:** Check actual gem names in PoE2 (may differ from PoE1)
- **Fix:** Edit spec file, swap for available gem
- **Fix:** Use weapon swap gems if primary gems are blocked
